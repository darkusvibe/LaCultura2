Wisdom in Kamba Proverbs

Ancient words that carry timeless truths guiding life, humility, and harmony.

Sayings of the Akamba

The Kamba people treasure proverbs as mirrors of wisdom. They teach patience, respect, and unity. One famous proverb says, “Utonyi wa mukamba ni wa ng’ondu” *The wisdom of a Kamba is like that of a sheep; quiet but deep.*

“A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step even the smallest goat starts by bleating.”

These proverbs are used to settle disputes, give counsel, and pass values to younger generations, showing how language can shape identity and moral strength.
