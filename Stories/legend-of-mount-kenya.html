The Legend of Mount Kenya

The sacred mountain where earth meets heaven, home of Ngai — the Creator.

The Mountain of God

Among the Kikuyu, Mount Kenya is known as Kirinyaga — the Mountain of Brightness. It is believed to be the dwelling place of Ngai, the Creator, who watches over the land from its snowy peaks.

“When you pray, face Kirinyaga, for Ngai dwells there.”

Birth of a People

According to legend, the first man and woman, Gikuyu and Mumbi, were placed by Ngai at the foot of the mountain. Their nine daughters became the founders of the Kikuyu clans, and the mountain remains the heart of spiritual connection for the community.

Echoes Through Time

To this day, elders pray facing Mount Kenya, and its silhouette is a symbol of national pride. It reminds all Kenyans that their roots lie in unity, faith, and respect for creation.
