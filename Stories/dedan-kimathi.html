Dedan Kimathi: The Spirit of the Mau Mau

The fearless leader who became the heartbeat of Kenya’s independence struggle.

The Flame of Resistance

Dedan Kimathi Waciuri was born in Nyeri in 1920. A teacher, farmer, and freedom fighter, he became the symbolic leader of the Mau Mau uprising against British colonial rule in the 1950s.

As commander of the Kenya Land and Freedom Army, Kimathi fought to reclaim land seized by settlers. His courage, charisma, and sense of purpose inspired thousands to rise up against oppression.

“Better to die on our feet than live on our knees.”

Legacy of a Hero

Even after his capture and execution in 1957, Kimathi’s spirit lived on as a symbol of sacrifice and patriotism. His vision of freedom fueled Kenya’s independence, and his name remains a rallying cry for justice and unity.
